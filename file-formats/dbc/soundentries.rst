Fields
------

soundType
~~~~~~~~~

-  ``0``: Unused/Miscellaneous,
-  ``1``: Spells,
-  ``2``: UI,
-  ``3``: Footsteps,
-  ``4``: Weapons/Impact,
-  ``6``: Weapons/Miss,
-  ``9``: Pick Up/Put Down,
-  ``10``: NPC Combat,
-  ``12``: Errors,
-  ``14``: Objects,
-  ``16``: Death,
-  ``17``: NPC Greetings,
-  ``18``: Test/Temporary,
-  ``19``: Armor/Foley,
-  ``20``: Footsteps,
-  ``21``: Water/Character,
-  ``22``: Water/Liquid,
-  ``23``: Tradeskills,
-  ``25``: Doodads,
-  ``26``: Spell Fizzle,
-  ``27``: NPC Loops,
-  ``28``: Zone Music,
-  ``29``: Emotes,
-  ``30``: Narration Music,
-  ``31``: Narration,
-  ``50``: Zone Ambience
